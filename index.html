about us

We are the Al-Atad company for storage and marketing of mixture, founded in 2014, specializing in the production and processing of mixture (food for farm animals) and also in importing,
Marketing and storage of grains and raw materials such as corn, wheat, barley, soybeans, sunflowers and more. The company owns a large fleet of trucks for transporting raw materials and
distributing the goods to customers. Our company owns large grain storage areas. Our company owns the newest Felix Feed factory for the production of dumpling mixture for cattle and
sheep established in 2021. The factory works to the highest standards accompanied by the most professional staff and nutritionists and under the supervision of the Ministry of Agriculture
Our professional team accompanies you throughout your journey in raising farm animals and provides you with any help you may need.

Our company opened a year ago the packing plant for hay bales of all types such as grain, oats, alfalfa, and hay mix after releasing the large bales and cutting the fiber to a length between 5
and 10 cm, cleaning and sifting from all harmful things such as stones and soil residues and then packing in plastic wrapped bales Compressed with a weight of 20 kg that can be kept in any
weather conditions without worry and will be served to the horses at any time as fresh supplementary food. Our company will always make sure to bring the best products to your animals and
we guarantee the cheapest prices. Our company has a large and experienced team in the field of animal husbandry and we will be happy to answer any questions or problems if any.
